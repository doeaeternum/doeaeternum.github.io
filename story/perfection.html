perfection 
			
The visitor in white leaves in silence,

while the visitor in red grins at you.

They begin to explain that you could only

choose one of the choices.

You say, yes... I knew that.

But they say that you didn't.

								
The visitor in red explains that the one you did

not choose, you will lose forever

because choices have consequences.

					
So now everything you do

is perfection, but all of your pride has vanished.

Well, isn't being prideful a deadly sin?

Good riddence, you say.

But is that really the case?

					
Without pride you cannot seem to find satisfaction
	
in anything you do, there is no sense of being proud 
	
of yourself or worthy of anything.
	
You begin to realize that perfection is worthless
	 
unless you have pride,

as long as you don't let your pride consume you.
