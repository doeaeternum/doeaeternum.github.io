peace_of_mind 
			
The visitor in red leaves in bitterness,

mumbling beneath their breath.

The visitor in white happily thanks you for choosing them.

They begin to explain that you could only

choose one of the choices.

You say, yes... I knew that.

But they say that you didn't.

				
The visitor in white explains that the one you did

not choose, you will lose forever

because choices have consequences.

				
So now you have a peace of mind for the rest of your life.

Well, you can't go wrong with peace in any shape or form... right?

				
Peace of mind is great, but part of having it is that once

achieved is being able to find happiness,

and now you won't be able to find that.

It is supposed to be part of the pursuit of happiness.

You can be at a peace of mind and accept things,

but what is it without happiness?
