You tell them thank you and ask them to get

into more detail about paying it forward that they spoke of.

They say that they weren't having a good day

So they decided to go out, despite the rain, and ended up

talking to a stranger who made them laugh and smile.

In that moment they said they wanted to make

someone else's day, even if it meant simply paying for gas.

The story warmed your heart.

You tell them that you'll pay it forward

when you get the chance to.

		

		
They thanked you, wait... They thanked, you?

They explain that they thanked you because of 

the choices that you made today

by not allowing the weather to dictate your day, and now

they can trust that you'll pay it forward in favor of another.
