You say thank you but no.

You tell them that you don't like accepting things...

That it makes you feel vunerable, selfish, or makes 

you feel obligated to give back, even if you can't.

	

	
They smile and say that feeling vulnerable makes

us human, that even accepting a compliment is still a gift.

It's not selfish as long as you appreciate it.

And that giving back doesn't always have to be in

the same way you received.

		

				
They tell you to enjoy the weather, because at least the rain

brings out a rainbow.
