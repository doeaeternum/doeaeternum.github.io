pride 

The visitor in white leaves in silence,

while the visitor in red grins at you.

They begin to explain that you could only

choose one of the choices.

You say, yes... I knew that.

But they say that you didn't.

								
The visitor in red explains that the one you did

not choose, you will lose forever

because choices have consequences.

						
So now you have to live your life with 

endless pride, but now you're missing perfection...

Which means there's flaws.

Now there's not anything wrong with flaws...

Unless they hold you back on improvement.

				
Nothing you do will never be perfect.

Which is fine, perfection cannot be reached...

but the issue is that you now have nothing to strive for.

You're too prideful in things that aren't up to par, but you

refuse to take advice or critique to improve.

You begin to realize that pride is nothing without perfection.
